Value Based Care

Healthcare systems around the world are currently threatened by challenges unique to our time; unsustainable expenditure, aging populations and the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases. The concept of value based healthcare proposes that payment for healthcare should be tied to the quality of care and the benefits it provides; switching from a ‘fee for service’ to a ‘fee for performance’ reimbursement model.

Innovative Pricing

Innovative pricing solutions such as outcomes-based or value-based pricing offer an increasingly dynamic solution to the uncertainty around the outcomes of treatment for patients. Such mechanisms reward manufacturers and providers for improving patient outcomes, incentivise innovation and encourage the transition to patient centric healthcare.

Pioneering Evidence Generation

Innovative evidence generation solutions are necessary to satisfy the essential requirement of relevant, timely and quality data to inform the provision of optimal value based healthcare. Circumventing the use of static or retrospective data will open the horizon to superior forecasting, trend analysis, business planning and oversight with regards to current developments in an increasingly dynamic industry such as healthcare.

The Digipharm Difference


Elimination of Barriers

Whilst value based healthcare provides the solution to numerous issues faced by healthcare systems, the extensive uptake of a value-based approach has been hindered by the infrastructural limitations of current health information systems, the administrative burden of its implementation and the fragmentation of health care delivery and patient outcomes data.

Patient Empowerment

Digipharm are strong believers in empowering patients with the custodianship and ownership of their personal data. Patients will be rewarded for sharing and contributing their health related data to inform decision making and health related research.

Blockchain Technology

Digipharms’ solutions will facilitate and accelerate the paradigm shift to value based healthcare delivery and the personalisation of medicine utilising ‘smart contracts’ and blockchain technology. Utilising blockchain technology for Digipharms’ platforms promotes the development of a decentralised health information infrastructure that is ubiquitous, transparent and security conscious.

Our Flagship Solutions


Reimburse

Reimburse will eliminate the infrastructural barriers and automate the implementation of value based pricing agreements, no matter how complex, on a fully independent global platform; encouraging personalised reimbursement of health technology on a ‘fee for performance’ basis for the mutual benefit of patients, payers and manufacturers.

  • Accelerates access to high cost therapy
  • Incentivises innovation
  • Drastically reduces administrative costs

Insights

Insights is the first ever ‘live’ real world evidence repository and allows the utilisation of real world evidence (RWE) as soon as it becomes available. The availability of ‘live’ RWE will provide stakeholders with real time intelligence with which they can inform strategic decisions and product development across the medicinal lifecycle.

  • Patient rewards
  • Revolutionises observational studies
  • Single platform for global evidence
