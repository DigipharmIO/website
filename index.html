The Digipharm Difference


Value Based Care

Healthcare systems around the world are currently threatened by challenges unique to our time; unsustainable expenditure, aging populations and the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases. The concept of value based healthcare proposes that payment for healthcare should be tied to the quality of care and the benefits it provides; switching from a ‘fee for service’ to a ‘fee for performance’ reimbursement model

Patient Empowerment

Whilst value based healthcare provides the solution to these issues faced by healthcare systems, the extensive uptake of a value based care approach to healthcare has been hindered by the infrastructural limitations of current health information systems, the administrative burden of implementing value based reimbursement agreements and the fragmentation of health care delivery and patient outcomes data.

Blockchain Technology

Digipharms’ solutions will facilitate and accelerate the paradigm shift to value based healthcare delivery and the personalisation of medicine utilising ‘smart contracts’ and blockchain technology with the aim of generating optimal patient outcomes relative to the monetary spend to obtain these outcomes. Digipharm are also dedicated to the development of innovative evidence generation solutions to satisfy the essential requirement of relevant, timely and quality data to inform the provision of optimal value based healthcare.

Our Flagship Solutions


Reimburse

Reimburse will eliminate the infrastructural barriers and automate the implementation of value based pricing agreements, no matter how complex, between payers and manufacturer on a fully independent platform; encouraging personalised reimbursement of health technology on a ‘fee for performance’ basis for the mutual benefit of patients, payers and manufacturers.

Insights

Insights is the first ever ‘live’ real world evidence repository and allows the utilisation of real world evidence (RWE) and real world data (RWD) as soon as it becomes available, to inform healthcare related decision making and health related research for a variety of stakeholders across the medicinal lifecycle while rewarding patients for contributing their data.
